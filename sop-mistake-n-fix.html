SOP Mistake & Fix

Content Hook Prompt
Write a short post.

Start with:
“Most people do [specific mistake] wrong.”

Then:
- Explain the mistake clearly
- Show the correct action step

Rules:
- Keep it practical and simple
- Avoid generic advice
- Make the fix immediately usable

End with a soft CTA (vary wording).
